# tidbits

Extracts tidbits of information from text.

What could be included?

* E-Mails
* Phone Numbers
* URLs
* Amounts of money
* Dates and times
* Legal citations
* Company numbers
* Person IDs
* Geonames
* IP addresses
* Acronyms
* ICD-10/ICD-9
* Bank account #, IBAN, BIC
* VAT IDs
* EAN/ISBN/ISSN
* License plates
